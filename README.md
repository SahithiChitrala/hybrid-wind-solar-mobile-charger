# Hybrid Wind and Solar Mobile Charger

Mini Project (GNITS, 2023) — designed and implemented a hybrid mobile charging system that integrates wind and solar energy sources to provide portable charging for electronic devices.

## What it does

* Generates electrical energy using both solar and wind sources
* Stores generated energy in a rechargeable battery
* Regulates output voltage using IC 7805
* Provides a stable 5V DC output for mobile charging
* Supports off-grid and portable charging applications

## Components Used

* Solar Panel (16V, 5W)
* Dynamo (12V, 500 RPM)
* Battery (12V)
* IC 7805 Voltage Regulator
* 1N4007 Diode
* 220Ω Resistors
* Wind Fan
* LEDs
* Charge Controller

## Working Principle

The system combines energy generated from a solar panel and a wind-driven dynamo. The generated energy is stored in a battery and regulated through an IC 7805 voltage regulator to provide a stable 5V output suitable for charging mobile devices. LEDs indicate power availability, while the charge controller manages charging and battery protection.
